Air Quality - Privacy Policy

When you use the Air Quality Alexa skill, you may provide permissions for the skill to access your postcode and city. This data is used to find your nearest air quality reporting station. If you choose not to provide this information, you can still use the Alexa skill by naming the place you wish to find air quality data for.

None of your personally identifiably information is stored by the Air Quality service, and you are free to revoke the permissions you grant the skill at any time through the Alexa platform.
